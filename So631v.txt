<h5 class='' id=''>Web Designing</h5>
<br />
<li class='list-group-item ' id=''>&nbsp;
	<p class="lead">
		<strong>Web Designing</strong> &nbsp;is the
		<strong>process of building and maintaining websites</strong>.
		<strong>Web Designing</strong> &nbsp;includes different aspects like
		<strong>Front End coding</strong>,
		<strong>website wireframe</strong>,
		<strong>website layout design</strong>,
		<strong>color combinations</strong>,
		<strong>fonts</strong>,
		<strong>graphic design</strong>,
		<strong>webpage interactions</strong>etc.</p> &nbsp;&nbsp;
</li>

		<strong>Web Designer</strong> &nbsp;build
		<strong>Visual</strong>,
		<strong>frontend</strong>/
		<strong>Client Side</strong> &nbsp;or
		<strong>User Interface</strong> &nbsp;part of a website.
		<strong>Front End</strong> &nbsp;includes writing
		<strong>mark up</strong>,
		<strong>stylesheet</strong>,
		<strong>images</strong>,

		<b>Interactions</b>, and the
